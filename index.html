Proud of it's journey

Established in 2016, Sniper Automation has over the years emerged as leading manufacturer in Chennai of All type of Industrial Automation Conveyor systems. Sniper Conveyors are engineered customized, trouble free services and are available in extensive ranges. Sniper Automation has comprehensive design and Manufacturing capabilities, and dedicated team of professionals to cater to all aspects of customer application and suitable solution provider to Southern Part of India like Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telungana, Kerala and Karnataka.

We’re passionate about serving our clients to the fullest, while continuously growing, learning, and innovating. With competencies across multiple industries, we offer our customers a more reliable supply chain and confidence in the long life of their program.
